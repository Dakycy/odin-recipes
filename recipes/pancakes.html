Pancakes

Description

Thin custard pancakes on kefir are a great breakfast or just an addition to tea or coffee. Delicious, tender and ruddy - such pancakes will brighten up even the gloomiest morning, especially if you add sweet toppings or filling to your taste!

Ingredients

  • 5 grams of salt
  • 15 grams of soda
  • 40 grams of sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 200 grams of flour
  • 500 ml of kefir
  • 40 grams of sunflower oil
  • sunflower oil for frying

Steps

  1. Add salt, soda, eggs, sugar and mix
  2. add 180 grams of kefir, mix
  3. add 50, 50 and 100 grams of flour, mix after each time
  4. add sunflower oil and mix
  5. add the rest of the kefir and mix
    6. the dough is ready, turn on the stove to medium power and fry the pancakes, add sunflower oil before each pancake
